# 🐸 King Frog Hat Quest

A **Mysterious Boy** has appeared deep in the Comodo caves, offering to help you craft the ![Frog Hat](img/5528.gif) **King Frog Hat [1]**! You can find him at the coordinates: `/navi beach_dun3 213/99`.
