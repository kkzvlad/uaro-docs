# Patch Notes - October 9, 2025

---

## Gameplay

- **@wb / @ws Commands**
  Final iteration and fixes for `@wb` / `@ws` implemented.

- **Warpra Cleanup**
  Warpra cleanup/alphabetical order implemented.

- **Item Sale and Drop Protection**
  All costumes, pet eggs and cards (to include equipment that is carded) can no longer be sold to NPCs or dropped.
  ```
  - Items at refine of 4 or above will also be excluded.
  - Manually added variant shoes, priest BG weapons, Dimonka mid/lowers and non-refinable uppers,
    evil/angel wing ears, blood sucker, angel spirit, robo eyes and other misc lower/mids.
  ```
!!! note "Item Protection"
    Protected items will not show as an option. This will prevent accidental sale of items to NPCs.

- **Killcount Revamp**
  ```
  - Now counts kills across all mobs without the need to track.
  - Use @killcount <mobid> to display info for your monster.
  - Using @killcount without a mobid will display your most previous mob selection.
  - Displays total kills, kills without a card, cards dropped (total).
  - Announcement now shows your card obtained and the kill you've obtained it at (sum of killcount
    between your last obtained card and most recent acquisition).
  ```

- **Pick Stone Skill**
  Removed overweight status block from `Pick Stone` skill.

- **Global Chat Restriction**
  Global chats now restricted to base level `20+`.

- **Emperium Breaker Announcement**
  Added last hit emperium breaker name to announcement on FE and SE castles.

- **Bloody Branch MVP Announcements**
  MVPs spawned via Bloody Branch no longer give a MVP kill announcement serverwide (still count towards overall
  MVP kills).

## NPC

- **Headgear & Costumes Display (WoE Maps Only)**
  Added new `Headgear & Costumes` `@settings` feature for **WoE maps only**.

  | Setting                | Description                                       |
  |------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
  | Show all HGs and Costumes | Display all headgears and costumes          |
  | Show Only Mine         | Only display your own headgears and costumes      |
  | Hide All               | Hide all headgears and costumes                   |
